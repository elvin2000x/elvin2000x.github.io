Questions

Before you book

What exactly do I get for the price?

One complete AI workflow, scoped on a call, built end to end and handed over with written documentation and a live walkthrough. It is a fixed scope per workflow starting at $1,500 USD, confirmed with you before any work begins.

What kind of task works best, and how do you know it fits?

Repetitive, rule based processes are the strongest fit: email sequences, content generation, research and reporting, or data cleanup. If you can describe the steps you repeat by hand, it is usually a good candidate, and on the scoping call I will tell you honestly whether automation is the right move before you commit.

How long does the build take?

It depends on the scope of the one workflow, which is why we agree the timeline on the call before any work starts. You will know the plan and the handover date up front, so there are no surprises.

Do I need technical skills to run it afterward?

No. The handover includes plain written documentation and a live walkthrough so you and your team can run and maintain the workflow without writing code or calling me back. You own it fully once it is delivered.

Why is this fixed scope instead of ongoing consulting?

One clearly defined deliverable keeps the price predictable and the outcome concrete. You get a finished, working automation you own outright, with no open ended retainer. If you have more tasks to automate later, we scope each one as its own build.

Can I see client case studies?

Not yet, and I would rather be straight about that than invent them. This is a productized service and client results will be published as engagements complete. For now the proof is the systems I have built and run myself: fifteen directories, thirteen games, a self-hosted platform and my own email engine, all built with AI in production.
