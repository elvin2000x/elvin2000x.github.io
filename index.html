About

I spent years inside the machines. Ads at Google, then engineering in fintech. These days I build for myself. I make the apps I wished existed and the games I wanted to play, and every so often something that only makes sense to me. I write the code, draw the art, and run the servers, solo, with AI as a genuine co-worker rather than a party trick. I also wrote a book about doing exactly that. Everything on this page came out of one head and one keyboard, mostly on nights and weekends.
