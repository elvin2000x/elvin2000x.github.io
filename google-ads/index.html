Before you book

Who is this for?

Local and small businesses that are already spending on Google Ads, or about to start, and are tired of wasted budget and agencies that do not seem to care. If you want an account run by one accountable person who treats your spend like it is his own, this is for you.

What does the $800 a month cover?

It covers the full management of your account. That includes building or rebuilding the campaigns, weekly optimization of keywords, bids, and budget, proper conversion tracking, and plain-language reporting. It is the fee for the work and the expertise, and it stays the same whether your ad budget is small or large.

Is my ad spend included in that price?

No, and that is by design. You fund your own ad spend directly through your account, separate from the $800 management fee. That keeps everything transparent, and it means your money and your account history always belong to you.

Do I own my Google Ads account?

Yes, completely. The account stays in your name with all of its data and history. If we ever stop working together, you keep everything and lose nothing.

Is there a long contract?

No. The engagement runs month to month with no long lock-in. I would rather earn your business every month by getting results than trap you in a contract you have outgrown.

What makes you different from other Google Ads freelancers?

I spent three years running paid search from inside Google Ads before ever managing a client account, so I understand the platform at a level most freelancers never reach. I also drove roughly $100,000 in six months through search at a Toronto fintech, so I know how to turn clicks into real revenue rather than just spending your budget.

What results can I expect, and how soon?

I will be straight with you. I do not promise a specific revenue number, because honest results depend on your margins, your market, and your budget. Early cleanups of wasted spend often show up within the first few weeks, and the stronger gains come as the account gathers data and the campaigns are refined month over month. What I commit to is building the account properly, cutting the waste, tracking honestly, and pushing your cost per result down over time.

Do you work with businesses outside Toronto?

Yes. I am based in Toronto and work remote, so I manage accounts for businesses wherever they are. The work happens inside your account and over calls and email, so location is never a barrier.
