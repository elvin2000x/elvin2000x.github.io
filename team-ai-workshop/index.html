Questions

Before you book

Is the workshop remote or in person?

I run it both ways. For remote and distributed teams I run it live over video, and for teams in the Toronto area I run it in person. Both formats are fully hands-on, with your team working through their own tasks in real time. The content and the outcome are the same either way.

How many people can join?

It is designed for a full team in one live session, from a handful of people up to a full department. If you have a large group or several departments, book a call and we will size the format so everyone still gets hands-on time.

How do you tailor it to our work?

Before the session I gather the tasks and workflows your team handles most, then we work through those live. The prompts and workflows you leave with match your real day to day.

Do we need any prior AI experience?

No prior experience is needed. The session meets your team where they are and gives everyone the same strong foundation, whether they use AI daily or have barely touched it. Because everything is built around your own tasks, it works just as well for complete beginners as for the people already experimenting.

How is this different from watching AI tutorials online?

Tutorials teach generic tricks that rarely fit your actual work. This session is built around your team's real tasks and leaves everyone on the same shared playbook, taught by someone who uses these tools in production every day.

How do we book?

Book a short call at meet.elvinpeters.com. We will confirm the format, the timing, and how I tailor the session to your team.
