Where your budget is quietly leaking

Most accounts that have been running for a while carry hidden waste. Broad keywords pull in searches that never convert. Conversion tracking reports numbers that do not reflect real sales. Bids push hard on terms that lose money and hold back on the ones that make it. You feel the symptom as a rising cost per lead while the cause stays buried in the account. The audit surfaces every one of these leaks and shows you which ones cost you the most.

  • Account and campaign structure that spreads budget too thin
  • Keywords and search terms draining spend with no return
  • Conversion tracking that misreports what is actually working
  • Quality issues quietly raising your cost per click
  • Bidding and budget settings working against your goals
